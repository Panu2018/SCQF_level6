Its uniqueness is that shops line only one side of the street, leaving the opposite side a lush and verdant green space giving shoppers breathtaking views of the Old Town cityscape. Located at the east end of Princes Street, you’ll find the newest edition to Edinburgh’s shopping centres – St James Quarter. From beauty to clothing, electronics to footwear, this 4-storey centre has brands in Scotland for the first time, such as & Other Stories, Stradivarius, Bershka, and Pull&Bear. The 1.7 million square feet also includes restaurants, cafés, bars, public spaces, leisure venues and a five-screen cinema. Why not make a stay of your trip – the centre also accommodates Roomzzz Aparthotels and the stylish 12-storey hotel, W Edinburgh.
